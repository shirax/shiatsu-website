About

shiatsu s a form of Japanese bodywork based on the theoretical framework of traditional Chinese medicine.

In the Japanese language, shiatsu means "finger pressure".

Shiatsu techniques include massages with fingers, thumbs, feet and palms; assisted stretching; and joint manipulation and mobilization.

Tali is a certified shiatsu practitioner, who combines her knowledge, intuition and heart when treating her patients.
